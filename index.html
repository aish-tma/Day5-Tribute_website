Biography

Abdul Sattar Edhi. The very name resonates with a profound and unwavering dedication to humanity that few have ever matched. For me, and for countless others, his life is not just a biography, but an inspiring testament to the power of empathy in action. Witnessing the suffering around him from a young age, particularly caring for his beloved mother, forged within him an unbreakable commitment to alleviate the pain of others. When he came to Pakistan, the visible hardship only intensified his resolve. With the purest of intentions and tireless effort, he built an empire of compassion from the ground up, brick by painstaking brick. His philosophy, so simple yet so profound – "No religion is higher than humanity" – became the guiding light for a movement that touched and saved countless lives. Edhi Sahib's legacy isn't just in the ambulances, the orphanages, or the shelters; it's in the unwavering example he set, a powerful reminder of what one person, driven by selfless love, can achieve. He wasn't just a humanitarian; he was the embodiment of humanity at its finest, and his impact continues to inspire us all.

Edhi Foundation

The Edhi Foundation, born from Abdul Sattar Edhi's vision in 1951, is Pakistan's largest welfare organization, offering free, non-discriminatory aid to the vulnerable. From its iconic ambulance service to shelters, hospitals, and burial assistance, the Foundation provides crucial support for the marginalized. Built on principles of impartiality and selfless service, it relies on public donations to deliver hope and dignity to those in need, embodying Edhi's lifelong dedication to humanity. It stands as a powerful and enduring legacy of compassion in action.

Services

  • Ambulance Service: A nationwide fleet of 1,500+ vehicles for emergency transport.
  • Edhi Homes: Over 330 shelters providing care for 8,500+ individuals.
  • Healthcare: 25 hospitals and 335+ centers offering free medical aid.
  • Burial Services: Dignified arrangements for the poor and unclaimed.
  • Adoption Services: Facilitating homes for 20,000+ abandoned children.
  • Women & Children Support: Sheltering victims of violence and neglect.
  • Food Distribution: Providing essential supplies to those in need.
  • Disaster Relief: Offering aid and support during national crises.
  • Missing Persons: Helping reunite thousands of families.
  • Rehabilitation: Supporting 50,000+ orphans with education and training.
